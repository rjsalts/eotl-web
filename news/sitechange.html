Site Change

As you may or may not know, we have lost our contact to AUS, the machine that houses EotL at Stanford University. As a result, EotL will be moving from Stanford to an undetermined location sometime in the near future. Other than some brief downtime during the move, the players and wizards here shouldn't be affected too much.

The one thing that is very important is that all EotLers begin using the address eotl.org 2010 to connect to the MUD. After the move we will lose access to Stanford's DNS records, which means the following addresses will not work:

    171.64.12.30 2010
    aus.stanford.edu 2010
    mud.stanford.edu 2010
    eotl.stanford.edu 2010

Again, the only surefire way for you to make a smooth transition to the machine change is to begin using the new address, eotl.org 2010 now. Sorry for the inconvenience.
The Administration.
