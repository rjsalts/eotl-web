EotL's Reimbursement Policy

Reminder:
As a general rule we don't reimburse player losses of any sort for any reason, because we would almost literally have to devote our entire lives to fixing people up...

If some wizard is nice enough to reimburse you for some major loss suffered from a bug or something, consider yourself lucky, and don't be surprised if you are turned down next time.

Demands for reimbursement will likely only annoy the wizards.

And don't bug me personally. I don't have the time (and lack the patience - you have been warned).

-Xurbax-
