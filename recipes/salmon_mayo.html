Mayo Salmon

contributed by Maera

This is really easy and very good.

The Recipe

Get some salmon fillets (not steaks). Spread some mayonnaise on top--a little more than a sandwich, but not too much. Sprinkle some dill and lemon pepper on top. I use fresh dill, but dried will work. Cook in the oven at 350F till done, I'd say about 25-30 minutes, depending on how flaky you like it.

It's good with asparagus on the side, too.
