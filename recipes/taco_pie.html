Taco Pie

contributed by Hodge

The Recipe

Take a pound of ground beef, fry it up and add taco seasoning. In a pie pan, line the bottom with a thing of pillsbury crescent dough and make a pie crust thing. Fill the bottom with about 1 cup of crushed Doritos, add the cooked and seasoned meat, top with a bunch of shredded cheddar, a couple plops of sour cream and then another half cup of crushed Doritos. Bake at 325F for about 20 minutes and then feast.

You can further top with lettuce and tomatoes and anything else.

Oh yah, nacho cheese Doritos, not cooler ranch. Wtf is cooler ranch, anyways?
