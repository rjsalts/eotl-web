Tahitian Chicken

contributed by Ahab

The Recipe

  • 3/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 3/4 cup flour
  • 6-8 chicken breasts
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup vinegar
  • 3 Tablespoons soy sauce
  • Cooked white rice

Heat oil in large frying pan. Flour the washed chicken and brown it in the hot oil. Meanwhile, mix the remaining ingredients, except rice, together in a small mixing bowl. Place chicken in a 9x13-inch baking dish, single layer. Pour soy mixture over chicken. Bake 1 hour at 350F. Serve with white rice.
