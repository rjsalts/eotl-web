Ramlette

contributed by Harpua

Good stuff for post-drinking breakfast, when you don't have the cash nor motivation to go to Waffle House.

The Recipe

  • 1 pkg ramen noodles
  • 1 T butter
  • 1-2 eggs

Cook the ramen as usual, but don't add the little spice packet! Drain the water after yer ramen is all cooked. Melt butter in skillet. Add noodles. Cook for a few mins, turning the noodles occasionally. Add spice packet and your eggs. You can mix the eggs up before in a small bowl if you're feeling ambitious. Cook until the eggs are done. Enjoy!

The Reviews

Hodge: It's ramen and eggs. Can't get much simpler than that. I don't really know what to rate this. It's not that great but I keep making it over and over when I don't want to put too much effort into eating. I give it a 5 for simplicity but a 2 on taste (it doesn't taste bad, it just doesn't taste great). It should be noted that I only buy chicken flavored ramen and I really don't like the flavor packets all that much. I'm currently experimenting with the amount of flavor packet I use. Last night I used about a quarter of one and I think it was too strong. I'll probably only use a couple shakes from now on, or none at all. Also, a bit of cheese grated over (or two huge chunks on the side) work really nice.
