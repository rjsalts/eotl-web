        The battle between the good of the Crusaders and the evil of the Dustmen has brought about the return of the ancient Druidic Order. Rumors abound regarding where the center of Druidic Power actually lies. Some say in the great forest of Baronar. Others say in the mountains of Entesia. Still others report that they have taken up residence with the elves. One thing is not to be denied, though: the druids have made their return, and will maintain the balance at almost any cost.

             -Shiriel
