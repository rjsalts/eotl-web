Linguist Guild

Although not a guild you can join, the linguist guild offers the diligent student access to all the various written and spoken languages of the realm.

Burbl
Spoken by the underwater races - squids, merfolk and the like
Darktongue
Spoken by the races of the deep recesses of the earth or dealings with the infernal - drow, imps, goblins or necromancers
Drazk
Spoken by dragons and their kin
Grwrl
Spoken by intelligent beasts - parthans, skaven and ka’nine
Klingon
Spoken by members of the Klingon empire and other spacefaring folk who have dealings with them
Silvari
Spoken by the sylvan races - elves, nymphs and pixies
Thorallen
Spoken by those who live beneath the earth - gnomes and dwarves
