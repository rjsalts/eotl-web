- Creating a New Character

When you connect to the game, you have three choices:

  1. Log in an existing character.
  2. Log in a Guest character (which is not saved upon quitting).
  3. Create a new character (see below).

Creating a New Character

Here is a quick run-through of the process of starting a brand-new character.

  • The first thing you will be asked for is your character's name. Names can contain only alphabetical characters (no numbers, spaces, or symbols) and the length must be greater than 1 and less than 15.
  • Second, new characters will be asked to enter their password twice (the second time for confirmation). The password must contain at least 6 characters. If the character already exists, you will get a simple Password: prompt.
  • Next, you will be asked to choose a race. You can get information on different races before you make your final choice.
  • Next, you will be asked to choose your gender.
  • Next, the starting stats of the race you chose will display and you will be given 3 free additional stat points to spend on any stat.

Once the character creation process is complete, you will start the game in the EotL Lounge, a safe haven protected from player killers and aggressive monsters, unless you chose the Android race.
